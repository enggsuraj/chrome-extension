Privacy Policy

Effective: April 28, 2026

CalmScreen does not collect any data. It has no backend. It does not phone home. All your settings stay on this device.

What we don't do

  • No analytics, telemetry, or "anonymous usage data."
  • No tracking pixels.
  • No ad networks. No third-party SDKs.
  • No A/B testing.
  • No remote configuration.
  • No cloud sync.

What CalmScreen stores locally

In chrome.storage.local, on your machine:

  • Your slider values (brightness, warmth).
  • Your selected preset.
  • Your list of disabled sites.

You can wipe everything by uninstalling the extension.

Permissions, explained

PermissionWhat it's for
storageSave your settings between sessions, locally.
scripting, activeTab, <all_urls>Apply the warmth + dim layer on the page you're viewing. CalmScreen does not read or transmit page contents — only adds visual overlays.

Your rights

Disable, uninstall, or wipe CalmScreen at any time. Because no data ever leaves your machine, there is no remote account to delete.

Questions? Open an issue on the CalmScreen source repository.
